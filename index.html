Who Are We 我们是谁

CoV Relief is a Canadian-registered not-for-profit organization. Our goal is to provide help, support and relief for the victims, infected people and affected residents of COVID-19 (Wuhan Coronavirus) outbreak.

CoV Relief 是一家在加拿大注册成立的非营利慈善组织。我们的目标是为 COVID-19（武汉冠状病毒）疫情的受害者、感染者和受影响的民众提供帮助与救济。尽可能给更多群众提供卫生健康环境，防止疫情扩散。

What Do We Provide 我们能做什么

We are providing support, including but not limit to

  • Purchase and distribute medical equipments and supplies, sanitizing supplies, and health support for infected area
  • Provide funding for victims or seriously infected people
  • Provide support for minor dependends or old-age people whose custodies are in forced isolation.
  • Provide support for community volunteers.

我们的行动包括并不仅限于

  • 为疫区提供卫生支持，包括医疗卫生用品的采购与分发
  • 为受害或受严重感染的群众提供资金支持
  • 为 COVID-19 造成的弱势家庭成员分离，尤其是老人与儿童，提供生活支持
  • 为社区义工提供物资及资金支持

How Does It Work 我们怎样做

We are partnered with companies and volunteers in China to purchase and distribute supplies, and provide health support.

我们与来自中国的伙伴单位和志愿者合作，采购物资并分发到疫区，并且提供卫生指导。

How To Contact Us

If you need help or you can provide help, please reach us through contact@covrelief.ca. We will follow up as soon as possible.

如果您需要帮助，或者您可以提供帮助，请发送邮件到 contact@covrelief.ca，我们会尽快与您取得联系。
