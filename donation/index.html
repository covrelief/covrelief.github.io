Fundrising 筹款活动

First round 第一轮

Goal: CAD $50,000

Expense:

  • Sanitizing supplies for critically infected cities.
  • Support minor dependants and old-age, whose custodies are in forced isolation.
  • Support community volunteers.

捐款目标：$50,000 加币

捐款使用方式：

  • 对疫情等级为“严重”的城市定点投放消毒物资
  • 资助双亲被隔离的儿童，或子女被隔离的无自理能力的老人
  • 资助社区义工

How to donate 如何捐款

Donation can be made by Interac e-Transfer through all major Canadian banks. Please send your donation to donation@covrelief.ca.

We may issue receipt for any donation transaction larger than CAD $10 amount.

To issue an official donation receipt for tax purposes, the following additional information is needed, as per CRA requirement.

  • Full name, including middle initial
  • Home address

Please send above information with your transaction reference number to donation@covrelief.ca

All donations will be published below. All expense will be published on "Expense" page.

我们接受加拿大银行的电子邮件转账捐款。请将您的捐款汇至 donation@covrelief.ca。

对于任何一笔超过 CAD $10 的捐款，我们均可开具正式的捐款收据，可用于抵税。依照 CRA 的要求，捐款收据上须包含以下信息：

  • 捐款者全名
  • 捐款者地址

如需要开具正式的捐款收据，请将以上信息发送至 donation@covrelief.ca。

所有的捐赠将会在下方“捐款清单”中更新。
